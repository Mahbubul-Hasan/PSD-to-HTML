Information about 1mg Calcium + Vitamin D3 Joint Health & Immunity Zinc, Magnesium and Alfalfa Tablet

1mg Calcium + Vitamin D3 Joint Health & Immunity Zinc, Magnesium and Alfalfa Tablet are beneficial for people who do not get enough calcium from their regular diet. While Calcium is necessary for normal functioning of nerves, cells, muscle, and bones. Vitamin D helps in the absorption of Calcium in your body. Having the right amount of Calcium and Vitamin D is important for strong bones. The Calcium and Vitamin D3 combination is also beneficial for women who are pregnant, nursing, or in their post-menopausal stage.
